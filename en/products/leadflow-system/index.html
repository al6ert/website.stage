How does it work?

Strategic, technical, and optimized from day one

  • Initial strategic briefing

    We define the value proposition, ad hook, goals, audiences, narrative, and assets. We align on the funnel approach and the qualification system.

  • Build and technical setup

    We create the funnel (forms or chatbots), configure advanced events, and connect your CRM. The copy and creative review process is straightforward and transparent.

  • Launch and daily optimization

    We activate campaigns on Google, Meta, or LinkedIn. We recommend a minimum of 3 months of management. It includes daily handling and review.

  • Reporting and continuous improvement

    Meeting after 15 days and then monthly. We deliver a clear report with actionable conclusions and integrated feedback from your sales or telemarketing team.

FAQs - Frequently asked questions

Is this only advertising or does it include a funnel?
It is a complete advertising acquisition project with an advanced funnel. Advertising is the main traffic source, but the system also includes segmentation, filtering, and qualification through forms or chatbots.
Do you work in your accounts or ours?
We always work in your own advertising accounts and digital assets. Full transparency and data 100% under your control. We analyze and optimize, but ownership stays with you.
Is using a chatbot mandatory?
No. The system can work with advanced forms or with a web chatbot and/or WhatsApp. It depends on your sales model and user type.
How long do I need to start seeing results?
We recommend a minimum of 3 months of management. During that time we optimize campaigns, adjust targeting, and fine-tune according to telemarketing feedback.
Does it integrate with my CRM?
Yes. We adapt the setup to your CRM and custom fields so data flows in a structured way and is ready to work with.
What makes this different from a basic campaign?
Here we do not send traffic straight to a simple form. We create a personalized user experience that filters, segments, and prioritizes before the sales contact happens.
